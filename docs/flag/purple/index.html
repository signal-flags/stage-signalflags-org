Purple flag

Beach safety

In a beach safety context a purple flag can indicate the presence of marine pests or pollution, or even a protected marine environment. The International Life Saving Federation gives the following description:

Marine pests, such as jellyfish, stingrays, sea snakes or other marine life which can cause minor injuries are present in the water. This flag is not intended to indicate the presence of sharks. In this latter case the red and white quartered flag should be hoisted.
